## The Story

Deep in the valley of **Sterntal** floats a great crystal called **Luminos**. Its gentle glow keeps all five lands in harmony: the rain falls softly over the hills, Easter flowers bloom on time, snow drifts down in perfect flakes, the city hums along cheerfully, and the sky islands hold together without too much wobbling.

One moonlit night, the ancient cloud spirit **Nebbio** drifted just a little too close, bumped into Luminos, and — *clonk* — shattered it into five **Dream Shards** that shot off in every direction. Nebbio felt terrible about the whole thing and fled into the clouds to think about what he'd done.

Without Luminos the five lands fell into disarray. The rain got aggressive. The Easter bunnies started hopping in the wrong direction. A permanent blizzard set in over the frozen lake. City traffic turned into complete chaos. And the sky islands began tilting at very inconvenient angles. Worst of all, the critters of each land grew confused and grumpy — wandering about, bumping into things, and generally making a nuisance of themselves.

They are **not** monsters, mind you. They're just having a bad day.

**Miro**, a cheerful violet dragon with golden horns and an inexhaustible supply of fireballs, decided enough was enough. Together with their quick-witted fox companion **Fynn** — who follows everywhere at a loyal trot and has a surprisingly effective pounce — Miro set off to recover the five Dream Shards and restore Luminos.

The shards don't respond to force. They respond to *generosity*. Each shard reveals itself only once every last coin in its land has been collected. So Miro and Fynn must explore every nook, peek behind every waterfall, and rummage through every snowdrift before the way forward opens.

Five worlds. Five shards. One dragon, one fox, and zero monsters.

## How to Play

| Action | Keys |
|--------|------|
| Move | Arrow Left / Right &nbsp;or&nbsp; A / D |
| Jump | Space &nbsp;or&nbsp; Arrow Up &nbsp;or&nbsp; W |
| Double-jump → glide | Jump again in the air (Kirby-style puff) |
| Deflate glide | Arrow Down &nbsp;or&nbsp; S |
| Shoot fireball | Z &nbsp;or&nbsp; X |
| Board / leave vehicle | Walk into it &nbsp;/&nbsp; press Up |

**Fynn** follows automatically and attacks nearby grumpy critters on his own — no button needed.

Collect all coins in a level to unlock the exit gate. Checkpoints appear automatically so you never have to go back far.

## Development

```bash
pnpm install
pnpm dev       # start dev server (Vite)
pnpm build     # production build
```

**Stack:** TypeScript · [Excalibur.js](https://excaliburjs.com/) · Vite · pnpm

All sprites are drawn procedurally in TypeScript (no external image assets required to run).
